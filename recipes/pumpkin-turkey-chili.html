Pumpkin Turkey Chili

Description

Pumpkin Turky Chili is a must in season (Halloween) with fresh flavor and only 40 minutes prep to table, Easily one of the tastiest and simplest pot to-go you'll make in season. Easy to prepare, Gluten free ofen described as the little spicy pot.

Ingridients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  • 1 clove gralic, minced
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground balck pepper
  • 1 dash salt
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream

Steps

  • Step 1

    Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, green bell pepper, yelllow bell pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until tender.

  • Step 2

    Stir in turkey and cook until evenly brown; drain.

  • Step 3

    Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin. Season with chili powder, pepper, and salt. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer with chili powder, pepper, and salt. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Server topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
